About Zero Sphere

Zero Sphere is an immersive VR game set in a zero-gravity environment. Developed by Snow Studios, this game offers a unique and thrilling experience with intuitive locomotion mechanics and exciting team-based gameplay.

Features

Zero-Gravity Gameplay

Navigate and interact in a zero-gravity space with precise control.

Dynamic Team Play

Join one of two teams and compete for victory in fast-paced matches.

Customizable Cosmetics

Personalize your character with a range of cosmetic options.
